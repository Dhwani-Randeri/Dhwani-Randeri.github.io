Death
Note

Death Note is a Japanese manga and anime series where a high school student named Light Yagami finds a supernatural notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. The series follows the cat-and-mouse game between Light and a mysterious detective named L as they try to outwit each other.

Your Lie
In April

Your Lie in April is a Japanese manga and anime series that follows the story of a talented but emotionally distant pianist named Kosei who meets a free-spirited violinist named Kaori and begins to learn the true meaning of love and music. The series explores themes of love, loss, and the power of music to heal and connect people.

Solo
Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel and manga series about a low-ranked hunter named Sung Jin-Woo who gains a power that allows him to level up quickly, becoming the strongest hunter in the world. The series follows Sung as he embarks on dangerous quests and battles powerful monsters, all while facing opposition from other hunters who fear his growing power.

One
Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga and anime series that follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy with rubber powers who sets out to become the King of the Pirates and find the legendary treasure known as "One Piece". The series takes place in a world of pirates, marines, and other characters as Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, journey through the Grand Line in search of the treasure and face obstacles and enemies along the way.

Tokyo
Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is a Japanese manga and anime series set in a world where humans coexist with supernatural creatures known as ghouls who must eat human flesh to survive. The series follows Ken Kaneki, a college student who becomes a half-ghoul after an encounter with a ghoul and must navigate his new life as a member of the ghoul society while facing both ghoul and human enemies.
